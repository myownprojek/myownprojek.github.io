How To Buy $BARFIGHT Token

STEP 1
Ensure you have a web3 compatible wallet with the BSC Mainnet added to it.
STEP 2
Send BNB (Binance Coin) to your web3 wallet. BNB can be purchased trough centralized exchanges such as Binance.com
STEP 3
Go to https://pancakeswap.finance/ and connect your web3 wallet. Click trade and swap $BNB to $BARFIGHT. $BARFIGHT should replace where it says "Cake. You can locate the token by searching for BARFIGHT or inputting the 0xAF7E10c6bBBd0993f1E3209176B9B6142814d6d4
STEP 4
Confirm how much $BNB you want to swap for $BARFIGHT and accept the transaction prompts in your wallet. Once confirmed you now own $BARFIGHT!
